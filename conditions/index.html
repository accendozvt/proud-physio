Conditions We Treat

Conditions We Treat with Physiotherapy

From neurological and orthopedic conditions to paediatric and women's health, explore what we treat and how physiotherapy can help you recover.

We treat a wide range of conditions across all ages, at our clinic and in your home. Select a condition to learn what it involves and exactly how our physiotherapy helps.

Neurological Conditions

Stroke

Rebuilding movement, balance and independence after a stroke.

Parkinson's Disease

Improving mobility, balance and confidence with movement therapy.

Spinal Cord Injuries

Maximising strength, function and independence after injury.

Neuropathies

Managing weakness, numbness and balance from nerve damage.

Hemiparesis

Restoring strength and control on a weakened side of the body.

Orthopedic Conditions

Back Pain

Relief and long-term recovery from acute and chronic back pain.

Neck Pain

Treating stiffness, tech neck and referred arm pain.

Knee Pain

From arthritis to injuries, restoring pain-free knee movement.

Ankle Sprain

Full recovery and prevention of repeat ankle injuries.

Tennis Elbow

Relieving elbow pain and rebuilding a strong, pain-free grip.

Sports Injuries

Safe return to sport after muscle, ligament and joint injuries.

Paediatric Conditions

Developmental Delay

Helping children reach movement milestones through play-based therapy.

Cerebral Palsy

Improving movement, posture and function for every child.

Muscular Dystrophy

Maintaining strength, mobility and independence for longer.

Down Syndrome

Supporting strength, tone and motor development in children.

Autism

Building coordination, motor skills and body awareness.

Women's Health Conditions

PCOS

Exercise and lifestyle physiotherapy to support PCOS management.

Diastasis Recti

Safely closing abdominal separation and rebuilding core strength.

Antenatal & Postnatal

Safe, expert care through pregnancy and recovery after birth.

Pelvic Floor Dysfunction

Treating incontinence, pelvic pain and pelvic floor weakness.
