Expert Physiotherapy, Without Leaving Home

Home care is not simply a clinic session moved indoors. Treating you in your real environment carries a genuine clinical advantage: we see the exact bed, bathroom, staircase and furniture you use every day, and we solve the problems that actually shape your recovery. A safe transfer from bed to chair, a handrail in the right place, a doorway wide enough for a walking frame, these details matter far more in practice than they ever do in theory.

For patients who are frail, in pain or newly out of hospital, avoiding a stressful, sometimes unsafe journey also means treatment can start earlier and continue more consistently, which is often exactly when it counts most.

Home visits are especially valuable in the first weeks after a stroke or major surgery, when travelling is unsafe but early, correct rehabilitation makes the biggest difference to the final outcome. For bedridden patients, regular physiotherapy at home helps prevent pressure sores, chest complications, joint stiffness and muscle wasting, protecting comfort and dignity as much as mobility.

The best place to recover is not always the clinic. Very often, it is the home a patient already knows and trusts.

What a Home Session Looks Like

Every visit follows the same careful structure as a clinic appointment, adapted to your surroundings. Here is how a typical session unfolds:

  1. Assessment in your real environment

    We evaluate strength, mobility, balance and daily function right where you live. Seeing your actual bed, bathroom, staircase and furniture lets us solve the problems that matter most, and flag simple safety changes on the spot.

  2. Hands-on treatment with portable equipment

    We bring what the session needs: resistance bands and weights, mobility aids, electrotherapy units for pain relief, and positioning supports. Your home becomes a fully equipped treatment room for the hour.

  3. Personalised exercise programme

    Every patient receives a structured, progressive programme using their own surroundings, so exercises continue safely between visits without special machines.

  4. Family and caregiver training

    We teach family members safe transfer techniques, positioning for bedridden patients and how to supervise exercises correctly, so progress continues every single day, not just on visit days.

Did you know? Studies show that home-based rehabilitation can be just as effective as clinic-based treatment for many conditions, including stroke and knee replacement recovery, and patients often stick to their programmes better in familiar surroundings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which areas of Kochi do you cover for home physiotherapy?

We cover Kakkanad, Vazhakkala, Thrikkakara, Edappally, Palarivattom, Kaloor, Infopark and surrounding areas of Kochi and Ernakulam. Call +91 80894 14419 to confirm availability for your location.

Do I need to arrange any equipment at home?

No. We bring the portable equipment each session requires, including resistance bands, weights and electrotherapy units. Your exercise programme is designed around items already in your home.

Is home physiotherapy as effective as visiting the clinic?

For most conditions, yes. Research shows home-based rehabilitation achieves comparable results to clinic treatment for stroke, joint replacement and elderly care, and treating you in your real environment often solves practical problems a clinic visit cannot.

Can you start home physiotherapy soon after hospital discharge?

Yes, and early is usually best. Once your surgeon or doctor allows movement, prompt guided rehabilitation improves the final outcome and reduces complications. We coordinate with your medical team and can often assess within a day or two of discharge.

Do you train family members to help between visits?

Yes. Caregiver training is a core part of every home visit. We teach safe transfers, correct positioning for bedridden patients and how to supervise exercises, so recovery continues safely on the days we are not there.
