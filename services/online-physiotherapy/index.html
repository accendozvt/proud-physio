Which Conditions Suit Online Physio

The honest answer is that most musculoskeletal and lifestyle-related problems respond very well to a well-run online programme. Video consultation is especially effective for:

  • Back and neck pain: assessment of posture and movement, then a graded exercise plan you can follow at home.
  • Posture problems: desk-related stiffness, rounded shoulders and forward head posture, corrected with guided routines.
  • Sports rehabilitation: progressing strength and mobility work after the acute phase of a muscle or joint injury.
  • Ergonomic advice: reviewing your actual desk, chair and screen setup live on camera and adjusting it.
  • Post-operative home programmes: continuing a surgeon-approved rehab plan safely between hospital visits.
Good to know: Some conditions genuinely need in-person, hands-on treatment, such as acute injuries requiring manual therapy, cases needing detailed physical testing, or anything where a clear diagnosis is not yet established. During your first video call we will tell you honestly if you would be better served by a clinic or home visit, and never stretch online care beyond what is safe.

How an Online Session Works

We have made the process as simple as possible, so that even patients who are not comfortable with technology can take part with ease. Here is how a typical journey unfolds:

  1. Booking and video link

    Call or message us to choose a convenient slot. We send a simple, secure video link that works on any phone, tablet or laptop, with no special software to install.

  2. Detailed assessment

    Dr. Noora asks about your history and goals, then guides you through specific movements on camera to pinpoint the problem, watching your posture, range and technique closely.

  3. Personalised exercise plan

    You receive a clear, tailored programme with the right exercises, sets and progressions, demonstrated live and explained so you know exactly how each one should feel.

  4. Progress reviews

    Regular follow-up calls check your technique, adjust the difficulty as you improve, and keep you accountable, often the biggest factor in a full recovery.

The exercises only work if they are done correctly and consistently. Online physiotherapy is built around exactly that: right technique, steady progression, real accountability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is online physiotherapy as effective as in-person treatment?

For many conditions, yes. Accurate assessment, the right exercises with correct technique, and consistent progression are the core of recovery, and all three work well over video. Where hands-on treatment is essential, we will recommend a clinic or home visit instead.

Can I have online physiotherapy from outside India?

Absolutely. We regularly consult with non-resident Indians and other patients living abroad. All you need is a stable internet connection and a device with a camera. We will simply agree a session time that works across the time difference.

What equipment do I need for a video session?

Very little. A smartphone, tablet or laptop with a camera, a stable connection, and enough space to move a few steps. We will let you know beforehand if any simple household items, such as a chair or a resistance band, would be helpful.

Which conditions are not suitable for online physiotherapy?

Acute injuries needing hands-on manual therapy, undiagnosed problems requiring detailed physical testing, and certain complex cases are better handled in person. During your first call we will assess this honestly and guide you to the right option.
