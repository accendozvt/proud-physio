🤔 Wondering why your lower back keeps aching?

Most lower back pain is non-specific, meaning it comes from muscles, joints and soft tissues, not anything sinister.
Common culprits: muscle strain, long hours sitting, disc irritation, sciatica and a weak core.
Good news: it usually settles with movement, and scans are rarely needed early.
Watch for red flags though, like leg weakness, numbness or bladder or bowel changes, which need urgent review.
