🤰 Wondering if it is safe to exercise during pregnancy?

For most healthy, uncomplicated pregnancies, gentle regular movement is safe and beneficial:
🔹 Eases back and pelvic pain, lifts mood, improves sleep and energy
🔹 Safe options: walking, swimming, stationary cycling, pelvic floor and gentle stretching
🔹 Avoid contact sports, high fall-risk activity and overheating
🔹 Always check with your doctor or obstetrician first
