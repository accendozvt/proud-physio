⚡ That electric pain shooting from your back down your leg? It could be sciatica.

Here is what you should know:
🔹 Sciatica is a symptom, not a diagnosis: irritation of the sciatic nerve, often from a disc bulge
🔹 Most cases settle over weeks with staying active and physiotherapy
🔹 Scans and surgery are rarely needed early on
🔹 Red flags needing emergency care: groin or saddle numbness, loss of bladder or bowel control, rapidly worsening leg weakness
