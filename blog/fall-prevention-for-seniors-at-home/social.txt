🏡 In our Kerala homes, caring for ageing parents is a shared family duty, and preventing falls is one of the most loving things we can do.

Falls are a leading cause of injury in older adults, but most are preventable.
A 2019 Cochrane review found balance and strength exercise cuts the rate of falls by about 23%.
Simple home fixes help too: good lighting, no loose rugs, grab rails, non-slip footwear, essentials within reach.
Review vision and medications with the doctor as well.
