💪 Can light weights really build muscle? With blood flow restriction (BFR) training, yes.

A cuff partially restricts blood flow while you train with light loads (around 20 to 40% of your max).
Systematic reviews show muscle growth similar to heavy lifting, a real win when heavy weights are not safe.
It is a valuable tool early after ACL surgery, for painful joints, and for older patients.
BFR must be done under trained supervision with proper screening, never solo from a video.
