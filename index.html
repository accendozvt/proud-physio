Welcome to Proud Physio & Wellness

At Proud Physio, we are dedicated to helping you recover faster, move with greater confidence, and live your life with less pain. Our team of expert physiotherapists are committed to providing personalised care for each and every patient.

Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing a chronic condition, or simply wanting to improve your physical performance, we're here to guide you through every step of your recovery journey.

Mission Statement

Our mission at Proud Physio and Wellness is to help each individual reach their full physical health potential and achieve an optimal quality of life. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality physiotherapy that is tailored to their specific needs, goals and personal circumstances.

The Healing Process

  • From sports injuries to chronic pain, we treat a full range of conditions.
  • Proud Physio, we're committed to delivering evidence-based treatments.
  • Trusted rehabilitation for complete recovery and long-term wellbeing.

Our Services

How We Can Help You Feel Better

+

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Our geriatric physiotherapy focuses on improving mobility, balance and independence for older adults, and helping restore function and reduce discomfort in daily activities.

+

Neurological Rehabilitation

We support people with neurological conditions such as stroke, multiple sclerosis, and Parkinson's disease through targeted movement therapy and rehabilitation.

+

Pediatric Physiotherapy

We treat children across a wide range of conditions with a focus on friendly, compassionate care designed for a child's unique stage of physical development.

+

Musculoskeletal & sports physiotherapy

Our musculoskeletal physiotherapy addresses issues stemming from joint, muscle and ligament problems. We work to restore function and relieve pain through targeted exercise, manual therapy and education.

+

Geriatric Physiotherapy

We provide specialised physiotherapy care for elderly patients with a focus on maintaining independence, mobility and quality of life through compassionate, evidence-based treatment and lifestyle guidance.

+

Ergonomic, nutrition & lifestyle guidance.

We provide ergonomic advice and nutritional methods and lifestyle strategies to support your overall health and wellbeing, and improve your physical and mental performance.

+

Women's health physiotherapy

We offer expert care with a specialist physiotherapy service that is designed to address a range of women's health conditions, from pelvic floor dysfunction to prenatal and postnatal support.

+

Recurrence Management

We help identify the root cause of recurring injury or pain, and work to reduce future injury risk through injury prevention programmes, education, movement analysis and rehabilitation.

+

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

We assist patients during their recovery after surgery to rebuild strength and balance, and restore confidence in movement through safe and guided rehabilitation exercise programmes.
