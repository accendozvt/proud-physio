FAQ

Physiotherapy in Kakkanad & Kochi — Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our physiotherapy and home care services. Can't find your answer? Reach out and our team in Kakkanad will be happy to help.

Who is the best physiotherapist in Kakkanad?
Proud Physio & Wellness in Kakkanad is one of the most trusted physiotherapy clinics in the area, with certified, experienced physiotherapists delivering personalised, evidence-based care across a wide range of conditions for patients of all ages.
Do you offer home care physiotherapy in Kochi?
Yes. We provide specialised home care physiotherapy across Kochi and Kakkanad for patients who are unable to travel. Our therapists bring the same expert, personalised treatment directly to your home so you can recover comfortably.
How long does physiotherapy recovery take?
Recovery time varies depending on your condition, its severity and your individual response to treatment. After your initial assessment, our physiotherapists will give you a realistic timeline and a personalised plan to help you recover as efficiently as possible.
How many physiotherapy sessions will I need?
It depends on your specific needs and goals. Some patients feel relief in just a few visits, while those with chronic or complex conditions may need ongoing care. We review your progress regularly and adjust your plan accordingly.
Do I need a doctor's referral to book an appointment?
No referral is needed. You can contact us directly to book a consultation and our team will guide you through the next steps of your recovery journey.
What conditions do you treat?
We treat back and neck pain, sports injuries, post-surgical recovery, stroke and neurological conditions, arthritis, paediatric and geriatric conditions, women's health concerns, posture problems and chronic pain, among many others.
Do you provide sports physiotherapy?
Yes. Our musculoskeletal & sports physiotherapy helps athletes and active individuals recover from joint, muscle and ligament injuries and return to peak performance through targeted exercise, manual therapy and education.
Can physiotherapy help with neurological conditions?
Absolutely. We support people with stroke, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease and other neurological conditions through targeted movement therapy and structured rehabilitation to restore function and independence.
Do you offer women's health physiotherapy?
Yes. Our specialist women's health service addresses pelvic floor dysfunction, prenatal and postnatal recovery, and other women's health conditions with sensitive, expert care.
Is physiotherapy available for children?
We provide paediatric physiotherapy with friendly, compassionate care tailored to each child's unique stage of physical development, treating a wide range of childhood conditions.
Do you treat elderly patients?
Yes. Our geriatric physiotherapy focuses on improving mobility, balance and independence for older adults, helping maintain quality of life through compassionate, evidence-based treatment.
Can physiotherapy help after surgery?
Post-surgical rehabilitation is one of our core services. We help you rebuild strength and balance and restore confidence in movement through safe, guided rehabilitation exercise programmes.
What are your clinic timings?
We are open every day from 08:00 AM to 09:00 PM, making it easy to schedule appointments around your work and family commitments.
Where is your physiotherapy clinic located?
Our clinic is located at SRA 26-C, Crash Rd, Vazhakkala, Kakkanad, Kochi, Kerala 682021, easily accessible from across Kochi and Ernakulam.
How do I book an appointment?
Simply call us at +91 80894 14419, email trispoir3@gmail.com, or use the "Book An Appointment" button on this page. Our team will confirm a convenient time for you.
Will my first physiotherapy session hurt?
Your first session is mostly an assessment. Treatment is always within your comfort level — our physiotherapists work with you to manage pain, not increase it, and explain every step along the way.
What should I wear to a physiotherapy appointment?
Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy movement and access to the area being treated, such as shorts or a t-shirt, so the therapist can assess and treat you effectively.
Can physiotherapy prevent future injuries?
Yes. Beyond recovery, we focus on prevention through injury-prevention programmes, movement analysis, education and strengthening strategies that keep your body strong, mobile and pain-free long term.
Do you offer ergonomic and lifestyle guidance?
We provide ergonomic advice along with nutrition and lifestyle strategies to support your overall health and wellbeing and improve both your physical and mental performance.
Why choose Proud Physio & Wellness in Kakkanad?
We combine certified, experienced physiotherapists with truly personalised, evidence-based care, flexible home-care options and a warm, supportive environment — so every patient gets the best possible recovery outcome in Kakkanad and Kochi.
